Swivori Privacy Policy

Swivori is a lightweight creator tool that helps users build image-generation prompts. This page explains how the app handles information.

Effective date: May 27, 2026

1. Who we are

Swivori is provided by Viori Studio. If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at privacy@swivhome.com.

2. What Swivori does

Swivori helps users turn short scene descriptions into structured image prompts. Users can write scene ideas, choose a style, and generate prompt text inside the app.

3. Prompt generation and user input

Swivori does not send the user’s scene descriptions or generated prompts to our own servers.

Prompt generation is performed locally on the user’s device.

4. Local storage

The app may store user-written scene descriptions, style selections, generated prompts, recent items, saved items, or app settings locally on the user’s device to support normal app usage.

This local data is not stored on a Viori Studio server.

5. Advertising

Swivori may display ads using the Google Mobile Ads SDK.

In the course of providing ads, Google may process certain information such as:

  • Advertising ID
  • App set ID
  • IP address
  • Device and diagnostic information
  • App interaction information related to advertising

Google’s handling of advertising-related data is governed by Google’s own privacy and advertising policies.

6. Accounts and server-side user data

Swivori does not require users to create an account.

Because we do not operate user accounts or store user prompt data on our own server, there is no server-side account data for us to delete upon request.

7. Children’s privacy

Swivori is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children through Viori Studio servers.

8. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will update the effective date on this page.
