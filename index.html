Swivori Privacy Policy

Swivori is a lightweight creator tool that helps users turn scene ideas into image-generation prompts.

Last updated: May 27, 2026

1. Developer and contact

Swivori is provided by Viori Studio.

For privacy-related questions, contact us at privacy@swivhome.com.

2. What Swivori does

Swivori helps users create structured prompts for image-generation tools. Users can enter short scene descriptions and generate prompt text that can be copied and used in other image-generation services.

3. Prompt generation and local processing

Swivori does not send the scene descriptions you enter or the prompts generated by the app to Viori Studio servers.

Prompt generation is handled on the device. Swivori does not operate its own server for storing, processing, or reviewing user prompt content.

4. Local app data

Swivori may store app preferences or locally saved prompt-related settings on your device, such as language settings, recent inputs, favorites, or similar convenience data if these features are available in the app.

This locally stored data is kept on your device and is not uploaded to Viori Studio servers.

You can remove local app data by using in-app deletion options where available, clearing the app data through your device settings, or uninstalling the app.

5. Ads and third-party processing

Swivori may use the Google Mobile Ads SDK to display ads.

When ads are shown, Google may process data such as advertising identifiers, IP address, device information, app interaction information, crash data, diagnostics, and other information needed to provide, measure, and improve advertising services.

Viori Studio does not control all data processing performed by Google through Google Mobile Ads. Please refer to Google’s own privacy and advertising information for details about how Google processes data.

6. No account creation

Swivori does not require users to create an account. Viori Studio does not provide a Swivori user account system for this app.

7. Server-stored user data

Because Swivori does not upload user prompt content to Viori Studio servers and does not require account creation, there is no Swivori account data or server-stored prompt content for users to request deletion from Viori Studio.

This does not include data that may be processed independently by third-party services such as Google Mobile Ads.

8. Children

Swivori is not specifically directed to children. If future app settings or store listings specify a particular age group, this policy may be updated accordingly.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if Swivori’s features, data handling, advertising behavior, or legal requirements change. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
