Swivori Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the Swivori mobile application (“Swivori”, “the app”), developed and provided by Viori Studio.

Developer and contact

Developer: Viori Studio
Privacy contact: privacy@swivhome.com

What Swivori does

Swivori is a prompt-making tool that helps users turn short scene descriptions, moods, styles, and visual ideas into structured prompts for image generation tools.

Prompt and scene data

Swivori does not send the scene descriptions entered by users or the prompts generated by the app to Viori Studio servers. Prompt generation is performed on the user’s device.

Swivori does not require users to create an account, sign in, or provide a name, email address, password, or profile information to use the app.

Local app data

The app may store app settings, language preferences, recent inputs, favorites, or similar app-use data locally on the user’s device, depending on the app version and features used. This data is used only to provide app functionality and convenience inside Swivori.

Locally stored app data can be removed by clearing the app’s data or uninstalling the app from the device. Because Viori Studio does not operate a server account system for Swivori, there is no server-stored account data for users to request deletion of.

Advertising

Swivori may use the Google Mobile Ads SDK to display advertisements. During ad delivery, Google and its advertising services may process information such as the advertising ID, app set ID, IP address, device information, app interactions, and diagnostic information.

This advertising-related data is processed by Google for purposes such as delivering ads, measuring ad performance, preventing abuse, and maintaining the advertising service. Google’s handling of this data is governed by Google’s own policies and controls.

Users may be able to reset or delete their advertising ID or manage ad personalization through their Android device settings, depending on the device and Android version.

Third-party services

Swivori may use the following third-party service:

  • Google Mobile Ads SDK / Google AdMob, for displaying advertisements.

If additional third-party services are added in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

Data sharing

Viori Studio does not sell user prompt content or user scene descriptions. Viori Studio does not share user-entered prompt or scene text with third parties through its own servers.

Advertising-related data may be processed by Google as described in the Advertising section above when ads are shown in the app.

Data retention and deletion

Viori Studio does not retain user prompt content on its own servers. Local app data remains on the user’s device unless the user deletes it, clears app data, or uninstalls the app.

Because Swivori does not provide user accounts and Viori Studio does not store user prompt content on its own servers, there is no server-side account or prompt database for Viori Studio to delete on request.

Children

Swivori is not designed as an app specifically directed to children. If this changes in the future, this Privacy Policy and the app’s store listing will be updated accordingly.

Security

Swivori is designed so that prompt generation happens locally on the user’s device. No method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, but Viori Studio limits its own data handling by not operating a server-side prompt storage or account system for Swivori.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

Viori Studio may update this Privacy Policy when Swivori’s features, third-party services, or legal requirements change. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.

Contact

For privacy-related questions about Swivori, contact Viori Studio at privacy@swivhome.com.
